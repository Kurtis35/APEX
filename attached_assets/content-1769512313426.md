## How to become a reseller

With over 20 years in the promotional product and branding space, Amrod is geared to working with our clients to supply good quality, well priced, expertly branded promotional products within industry-leading turnaround times.

As a strictly trade-only supplier, Amrod does not supply end users and only supplies registered and approved resellers.

Step 1

### Ensure you meet the requirements

As a strictly trade-only supplier, Amrod does not supply end users (companies buying for their own internal use of the product) and only supplies registered and approved resellers (companies buying for the purpose of reselling the products).

[WATCH THE VIDEO TO LEARN MORE](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CUPr9f1o8kM)

![Step 1 Image](https://assets.promoafrica.com/webassets/home/images/step-1.png)

Step 2

### Complete the registration form

Gather the necessary documents and complete the online registration form. Once we have received your information, it will be vetted to verify that you qualify as a promotional products reseller.

[COMPLETE REGISTRATION FORM](https://new.amrod.co.za/register)

![Step 2 Image](https://assets.promoafrica.com/webassets/home/images/step-2.png)

Step 3

### Pay your registration fee

For clients based in South Africa: if your application is approved, a once-off administration fee is payable. You will also receive a complimentary welcome pack that includes branded samples, which can be used as a selling tool to show your clients the various branding methods available.
